About Me

Hi, I’m Mohaned Magdy, a passionate graphic designer and web developer dedicated to crafting innovative designs and user-friendly web experiences. With expertise in creating solutions that merge creativity and functionality, I aim to elevate brands and help businesses grow.

My Recent Graphic Projects

Graphic Design for Gala Weddings

For this project, I had the opportunity to design promotional materials for Gala Weddings' exclusive home event packages. The design features a sophisticated and modern aesthetic, blending elegant visuals with clear, concise messaging. The color scheme utilizes soft neutrals and dark tones, evoking a sense of luxury and exclusivity, while the dynamic composition directs attention to key details like the event offerings and price.

Graphic Design for Fitness Gym

I created a dynamic and balanced layout to showcase key services like personal training, group classes, and memberships. High-impact images of gym equipment and people engaged in workouts are used to evoke motivation, while the clear typography ensures easy readability. The inclusion of action-oriented phrases and call-to-action buttons encourages potential clients to join or inquire about the gym’s services.

Graphic Design for Kempinski Cafe & Playstation Lounge

For this project, I designed promotional materials for Kempinski Cafe & Playstation Lounge, a sophisticated venue offering a blend of high-end dining and interactive gaming experiences. The design reflects the fusion of luxury and fun, with a color palette of deep, rich tones like gold, black, and dark maroon, evoking elegance and exclusivity, while incorporating modern, playful elements to highlight the gaming aspect.
